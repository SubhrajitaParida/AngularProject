Medical Tips

  • Eat a balanced diet rich in fruits and vegetables to support your immune system.
  • Stay hydrated by drinking plenty of water throughout the day.
  • Cover your nose and mouth with a tissue or your elbow when you cough or sneeze.
  • Exercise regularly to maintain a healthy body and mind.
  • Avoid close contact with individuals who are sick or having symptoms of illness.
  • Seek medical advice if you have concerns about your health.
  • Reduce stress through techniques such as deep breathing, meditation, yoga.
  • Protect your skin from the sun by wearing sunscreen and protective clothing.
  • Limit processed foods, sugary snacks, and beverages to maintain a healthy weight
