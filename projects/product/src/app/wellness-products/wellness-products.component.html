Wellness Products

Nutritional Supplements

  • Vitamin C capsules

    These capsules are commonly used as a dietary supplement to boost the immune system, particularly during cold and flu seasons. They provide a concentrated dose of Vitamin C, which is essential for overall health and immunity.

  • Omega-3 fish oil supplements

    These supplements contain essential fatty acids, such as EPA and DHA, derived from fish oil. They are beneficial for heart health, brain function, and reducing inflammation. Omega-3 supplements are often recommended for individuals who do not consume enough fatty fish in their diet.

Fitness Equipment

  • Yoga mat

    A yoga mat provides a comfortable and stable surface for practicing yoga, Pilates, stretching, and other floor exercises. It helps cushion joints and prevents slipping during workouts.

  • Dumbbells set

    Dumbbells are versatile strength training tools used to target various muscle groups. They can be used for exercises such as bicep curls, shoulder presses, lunges, and more. Adjustable dumbbell sets allow users to change the weight to suit their fitness level and exercise routine.

Personal Care Products

  • Natural facial cleanser

    This type of facial cleanser is made with natural ingredients and is free from harsh chemicals. It gently removes dirt, oil, and makeup without stripping the skin's natural oils, leaving the skin clean, refreshed, and balanced.

Aromatherapy

  • Lavender essential oil

    Lavender essential oil is known for its calming and relaxing properties. It can be used in aromatherapy to promote relaxation, reduce stress and anxiety, and improve sleep quality. Lavender oil can also be diluted and applied topically to the skin for its soothing effects.

  • Aromatherapy diffuser

    An aromatherapy diffuser is a device that disperses essential oils into the air, creating a pleasant aroma and providing therapeutic benefits. Diffusers come in various types, including ultrasonic, nebulizing, and heat-based diffusers, and they are used to freshen the air, improve mood, and promote relaxation.
